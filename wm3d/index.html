WM3D: A Native-3D Action-Conditioned World Model

Most world models predict the future in 2D video. WM3D predicts it in explicit 3D geometry — world points, camera pose, and depth — and conditions that prediction on the robot's action.

Positioning

Why native 3D?

A single video frame is consistent with many different 3D structures and camera motions. In 2D, the link between action and geometry stays implicit. WM3D makes the 3D structure itself the thing being predicted.

2D / video latent

The mainstream

Dreamer, GAIA, Genie, DIAMOND/IRIS, Sora-as-world-model. The state is a video latent or RGB frame — at most depth-augmented 2.5D. Action→geometry causality is a byproduct of predicting texture.

native 3D

WM3D

The state is explicit 3D — world points (pointmap) + camera pose + depth. The model predicts where the action moves the scene's 3D structure, so action→geometry is a direct training target, not a side effect of pixels.

The differentiator: using VGGT-style feed-forward 3D (pointmap + pose) as the world model's state and supervision target, and wiring it as the world engine of a vision-language-action closed loop.
Method

One dual-stream trunk, many heads

A frozen VGGT turns each observation into native-3D tokens. A dual-stream trunk predicts the future 3D tokens, conditioned on an action chunk. Pluggable heads land that prediction as geometry, pixels, scores, proposals, or video.

Results · text → video generation

Generating video from noise, steered by the world model

The final piece: instead of refining the rough prediction, HunyuanVideo generates the clip from pure noise (30 denoising steps) — its DiT steered by our trained control adapter, conditioned on the task text and the world model's predicted future.

Capability Text → video generation, from pure noise
in: task text + the world model's predicted future (no rough pixels at inference) → out: a full video generated from noise (30 steps, control scale 1.0)

Full text-to-video — no rough pixels are fed in at inference; the world model's prediction enters only through the control adapter. Prompt: "robot manipulation scene … visible object and gripper motion."

Pure text → video on LIBERO tasks

Drop the world-model conditioning entirely (control scale 0.0) and the same path becomes a plain text-to-video generator: the instruction is the only input. Each clip below is generated from noise for a distinct LIBERO command (captioned on top).

control scale 0.0 hands the clip entirely to the text path — useful as an optional pure-generation mode. Turning the world-model conditioning back up trades text freedom for scene/action grounding.

Status & roadmap

Trunk trained from scratch to ~1B params through the full staged pipeline on 160k OXE windows, with high-quality future prediction.

◐ Native-3D variant

The counterfactual claim-gate protocol is ready and the world-points + pose variant is training now; full action→geometry results follow on convergence.

◐ Closed loop

Runner, online tokenizer and action protocol verified by expert replay (success 1.0). Learned closed-loop success rate is the next milestone.
